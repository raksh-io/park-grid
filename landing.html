FAQs

Everything you need to know about ParkGrid.

Booking is simple! Log in to your dashboard, select an available slot (highlighted in green), choose your duration, and confirm your booking. You'll be redirected to a secure payment page to finalize your reservation.
If you overstay, your slot status will change to "Overstay" and a fine will start accumulating. You can resolve this by clicking the "Pay Fine & Leave" button on your dashboard, which will take you to the payment page for the outstanding fine.
Yes, absolutely. We use Razorpay, one of the most trusted payment gateways, to handle all transactions. ParkGrid never stores your credit card details or sensitive payment information on our servers.
Currently, once a slot is booked and occupied, it remains ours until the duration expires or you manually leave. We are working on a refund-eligible cancellation policy for future updates.
Our system constantly monitors reservation times. If a slot is not vacated by the end of the booked period, it enters overstay mode. Once the user leaves or the admin releases the slot, it becomes immediately available for other users.
